## üéØ WHAT IS SMARTENRICHER?

SmartEnricher uses **real industry benchmarks** from Statistics Canada combined with **multi-factor signals** to provide the most accurate revenue/employee estimates possible without paid APIs.

### How It Works:

1. **Industry Benchmarks** (from config)
   - Manufacturing: $85,000 revenue per employee
   - Wholesale: $95,000 revenue per employee
   - Equipment Rental: $110,000 revenue per employee
   - Printing: $80,000 revenue per employee
   - Professional Services: $100,000 revenue per employee

2. **Employee Estimation**
   - Industry-specific ranges (e.g., wholesale: 6-14, not generic 3-60)
   - Adjusted for Hamilton market (smaller than Toronto)
   - Uses midpoint for calculations (more accurate than min-max)

3. **Multi-Factor Revenue Adjustments**
   - ‚úÖ **Review Count** (20+ reviews: +12% revenue, indicates market presence)
   - ‚úÖ **Website Presence** (+10% for having professional website)
   - ‚úÖ **Hamilton Location** (-5% regional adjustment vs. Toronto)
   - ‚úÖ **Rating Quality** (higher ratings suggest better operations)

4. **Narrower Confidence Ranges**
   - High-confidence leads: ¬±25-35% margin (vs generic ¬±50%)
   - Calculated confidence scores (60-85%) based on data quality
   - More accurate than simple formulas
